  • /e/: get, well, let, yes, when, tell, them, then, never, said, help, again, friend, better, everything, ever, hello, remember, left, head
  • /æ/: that, have, can, at, as, back, had, man, thank, happen, ask, after, than, last, hand, dad, bad, family, matter, actually

10 -s 和 -ed 尾音

  • -s规则示例：
    • /s/: books, stops, helps, thinks, laughs
    • /z/: dogs, sees, plays, comes, lives
    • /ɪz/: boxes, watches, misses, catches, changes
  • -ed规则示例：
    • /t/: worked, stopped, helped, watched, laughed
    • /d/: played, called, loved, lived, studied
    • /ɪd/: wanted, needed, started, decided, visited

11-21 节奏与语调词汇

  • 重音练习词：photograph/photographer/photographic
  • 连读练习词：pick up, not at all, go out, look at it
  • 弱读功能词：a, an, the, to, for, of, and, but, or, can, will, would
  • 焦点词练习：really, absolutely, definitely, completely
  • 语调练习词：yes, no, maybe, perhaps, however, therefore
  • 缩略形式：I'm, you're, he's, she's, it's, we're, they're, can't, won't, wouldn't

使用提示：

  1. 每个音标组合5-10个高频词进行集中练习
  2. 标注音标帮助准确发音
  3. 使用最小对立对进行对比训练
  4. 按照从易到难的顺序排列单词
  5. 可根据学员水平调整词汇难度
