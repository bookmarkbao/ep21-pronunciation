11月2日

  • Hey guys! This month I'm doing a 21-day live stream to challenge myself to improve my pronunciation.
  • Today is Day 2. Yesterday was Day 1, and I practiced /n/ and /ŋ/. Today I'm gonna practice more sounds.
  • So welcome back after 21 days and see how my pronunciation is going!

  • /t/

    • 单词：to, it, that, what, not, but, get, just, right, want, out, about, at, got, let, time, take, tell, too, talk

  • /d/

    • 单词：and, do, good, did, would, had, need, could, down, day, should, said, find, god, friend, told, doe, old, around, kid

  • /ʃ/

    • 单词：she, should, sure, show, wish, shut, shot, shall, shoot, finish, picture, special, ship, push, share, fish, station, shop, short, information

  • /ʒ/

    • 单词：decision, pleasure, usual, treasure, television, explosion, vision, measure, garage, version, occasion, division, conclusion, massage, illusion, visual, invasion, confusion, casualty, sabotage

  • /tʃ/

    • 单词：much, which, change, watch, actually, such, check, question, each, chance, children, child, touch, chuckle, catch, charge, cheer, future, choice, reach

  • /dʒ/

    • 单词：just, job, change, jack, charge, age, join, enjoy, gentlemen, imagine, joke, message, general, strange, judge, jump, agent, marriage, dangerous, major

11月1日

day1 /n/ 和 /ŋ/

  • /n/

    • 单词：and, in, on, no, not, know, can, want, one, now, when, an, then, been, need, man, mean, down, never, only

  • /ŋ/

    • 单词：thing, something, ring, bring, sing, king, morning, long, wrong, along, strong, song, hang, young, darling, think, thank, drink, bank, uncle
  • /dʒ/ 发音方法：
  1. 舌位： 舌尖抵住上齿龈（类似发 /d/ 的位置）
  2. 发音过程： 先憋气（像发 /d/），然后快速释放气流，同时舌头移向 /ʒ/ 的位置
  3. 声带： 震动（浊辅音）⭐ - 把手放喉咙上能感觉到震动
  4. 口诀： "d + zh = j" - 像中文"之"的声母加 /d/
