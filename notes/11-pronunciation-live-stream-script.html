Simple Pronunciation Practice Live Stream Script

Quick Intro

Last month: 21 days getting used to live streaming This month: 21 days practicing pronunciation

Daily Structure (30 minutes)

Opening (2 minutes)

Hello everyone! Day [X] of pronunciation practice! Today's sound: [e.g., /i:/ sound] Let's practice together!

Practice Session (25 minutes)

Just repeat after me:

  • Single sounds (10 times)
  • Words with the sound (10 times each)
  • Short phrases (5 times each)
  • Keep repeating until it feels natural

Example:

  • /i:/ ... /i:/ ... /i:/ ... (repeat 10x)
  • see ... see ... see ... (repeat 10x)
  • eat ... eat ... eat ... (repeat 10x)
  • I see you ... I see you ... (repeat 5x)
  • Please eat ... Please eat ... (repeat 5x)

Closing (3 minutes)

Good practice today! Tomorrow: [next sound] Keep practicing! See you tomorrow!

That's it!

  • Focus: 90% practice, 10% talking
  • Method: Repeat, repeat, repeat
  • Goal: Make the sound natural through repetition
