4) You're coming, ↗ aren't you? ↘ (反义疑问句)

## 21 发音的刻意练习方法
1) Listen and repeat. (回音法)
2) Record yourself speaking. (录音对比)
3) Shadow native speakers. (影子跟读)
4) Read aloud daily. (朗读练习)
5) Focus on weak areas. (重点突破)
6) Practice minimal pairs. (最小对立对)
7) Use rhythm drills. (节奏训练)
8) Imitate intonation patterns. (语调模仿)
